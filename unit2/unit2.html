Let's take a look at just a few of the most popular drinks that can be consumed both hot & cold.

There are a variety of drinks that can either be iced, or warmed depending on the occasion.
Let's take a look at a listing, as well as some of the classifactions to go along.

 

Below are some of the most popular drinks that can be served both hot and cold in no specific order, as well as some options to choose from.

 
  1. Coffee.
    • Iced latte (can be both hot & cold).
    • Hot black coffee.
    • Cappuccino (also can be both hot & cold).

  2. Tea.
    • Black iced tea (traditionally only seved cold).
    • Green tea (can be both hot & cold).
    • Southern sweet tea.

  3. Apple Cider.
    • Redfield.
    • Somerset redstreak.
    • Virginia crab.
